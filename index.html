The Story

Inspired by classic run-and-whip platformers like Cactus McCoy.

At dawn, the people of Eugene woke to terrible news: the legendary Golden Duck Statue had vanished from Hayward Field. Witnesses reported a shadowy bandit fleeing south toward the mist-covered summit of Spencer Butte.

The trail runs along the Willamette River, over Skinner Butte, through the Saturday Market crowds, past the roaring stands of Autzen Stadium, and up the steep forest switchbacks of the butte itself.

The city needs a hero. Will it be Meg... or Lily & Mako?

Choose Your Hero

Two playable characters, each with their own strengths.

The Powerhouse

Meg

Tough as old-growth timber and twice as stubborn. Meg has trekked every trail in Lane County and isn't about to let some bandit ruin track season.

⚡ Perk: stronger whip attacks + starts with an extra heart

The Scout

Lily & Mako

Quick on her feet with a sixth sense for treasure — mostly because her cat Mako does the sniffing. Where Lily goes, Mako follows.

🐈 Perk: Mako tags along and digs up bonus Duck Bucks

The Trail Across Eugene

Six real Eugene landmarks, one continuous side-scrolling world.

  1. 🌊

    Willamette Riverfront

    The journey begins along the river. Watch your step — those gaps are colder than they look.

  2. ⛰️

    Skinner Butte

    The hill that watches over downtown. A friendly hiker offers your first choice of the journey.

  3. 🎪

    Eugene Saturday Market

    Music, stalls, and fresh bread. Trade with a vendor — soup or a lucky tie-dye bandana?

  4. 🏟️

    Hayward Field

    The scene of the crime. A coach offers training: sprint speed or high-jump hops.

  5. 🦆

    Autzen Stadium

    It never rains at Autzen Stadium... but the rain clouds out here didn't get the memo.

  6. 🏆

    Spencer Butte Summit

    The final climb through the firs. The Golden Duck glints at the top. One last push!
