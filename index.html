The Story

A run-and-whip western platformer in the style of the classics.

At dawn, the people of Eugene woke to terrible news: the legendary Golden Duck Statue had vanished from Hayward Field. The only clues? A trail of paw prints, a suspicious amount of shed fur, and one chewed-up hair tie.

Every clue points to Mako — Lily's own cat — who has fled south toward the dusty summit of Spencer Butte with a gang of look-alike decoy cats covering his escape.

Meg wants the reward. Lily just wants her baby boy home before dinner.
